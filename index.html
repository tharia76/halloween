Alien Spaceship Escape

Mission Briefing:

Alert! You’ve been captured by an alien spacecraft. The ship’s systems are malfunctioning, and you’re trapped inside!

The ship's airlock is sealed, and the oxygen levels are dropping fast. Scattered around the spaceship are color-coded tubes, each labeled with a number.

Your task: Locate the hidden tubes and use the numbers on them to find the correct combination to unlock the airlock.

Time is running out. Find the clues, unlock the door, and escape the alien ship!
